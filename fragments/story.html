How It All Began

A little chapter from Kalveen and Nika — written for this demo, but told with a full heart.

Where it started

They met on a rainy afternoon at a small café in Tagaytay, both reaching for the last slice of sans rival at the counter. What began as laughter over dessert turned into long conversations about family, dreams, and the places they still wanted to see together.

For months they were friends who checked in after work, shared playlists, and walked the highlands on weekends. Somewhere between sunset drives and quiet prayers, friendship became something deeper — steady, patient, and sure.

The question

On a clear December evening, Kalveen asked Nika to marry him at the same lookout where they had watched their first sunrise as a couple. She said yes before he could finish the sentence, and they celebrated that night with hot chocolate and happy tears.

Now they are ready to begin forever — not as two paths that met by chance, but as partners who chose each other every day along the way.

With love,
Kalveen & Nika
